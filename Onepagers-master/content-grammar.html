Essential grammar for UX

These grammar choices directly impact user understanding and task completion.

Use active voice

Passive (unclear):

"Your password has been reset."

Who reset it? When? What should I do now?

Active (clear):

"We reset your password. Check your email for next steps."

Clear actor, clear action, clear next step.

Use clear pronouns

Ambiguous:

"Delete this item? This cannot be undone."

What does "this" refer to?

Specific:

"Delete this photo? You cannot undo this action."

Clear object, clear consequence.

Frame positively

Negative framing:

"Don't enter your password incorrectly."

Focuses on what not to do.

Positive framing:

"Enter your password carefully."

Focuses on the right action.

Stay consistent with tense

Mixed tenses:

"Save your work. Your changes were saved automatically."

Present and past tense mixed confusingly.

Consistent:

"Save your work. Your changes save automatically."

Present tense throughout creates clarity.

Content structure that works

How you organize and format content affects how quickly users can find and understand information.

1. Clear information hierarchy

Poor hierarchy:

Welcome to our platform here are some features you can use our analytics dashboard provides insights our collaboration tools help teams work together our security features keep your data safe contact support if you need help

Wall of text with no clear structure.

Clear hierarchy:

Welcome to our platform

Analytics: Get insights into your data

Collaboration: Work better with your team

Security: Keep your information safe

Support: Get help when you need it

Scannable structure with clear categories.

2. Chunk information appropriately

The magic number: 5±2

Research shows people can effectively process about 5-7 pieces of information at once. Use this for:

Navigation menus

5-7 main sections maximum

Form sections

Group related fields together

Feature lists

Highlight 3-5 key benefits

3. Make text scannable

Hard to scan:

Our service includes many benefits such as 24/7 customer support which means you can always reach us, unlimited storage so you never run out of space, advanced security features that protect your data, collaboration tools for working with teams, and detailed analytics to understand your usage patterns and optimize your workflow.

Easy to scan:

24/7 support — Always here when you need us

Unlimited storage — Never run out of space

Advanced security — Your data stays protected

Team collaboration — Work better together

Detailed analytics — Understand your usage

Content quality checklist

Use this checklist to review your content before publishing.

✅ Writing style

  • • Use sentence case for headings and labels
  • • Write in active voice
  • • Use consistent verb tense
  • • Choose specific over vague language
  • • Frame instructions positively

✅ Content structure

  • • Create clear information hierarchy
  • • Chunk information into digestible pieces
  • • Use bullets and formatting for scannability
  • • Lead with the most important information
  • • Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)

Remember: Every word is a design decision that affects user experience.
